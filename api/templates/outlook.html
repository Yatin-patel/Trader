How this page works

Universe: a union of (S&P large-caps, this project's scanner watchlist, tickers you've already traded). Capped at ~60.

Composite score is an equal-weighted blend of normalized realized P&L, win rate, trailing momentum (1M / 3M / 6M), and current IV rank. Higher = better wheel candidate right now.

30 / 60 / 90-day outlook (per ticker) combines two views:

  • Quant — projects price bands using a lognormal model fit to ~1 year of daily returns. Shows median, 10th-90th percentile band, and probability of finishing above current price.
  • LLM narrative — Gemini/Claude writes one paragraph per horizon, grounded in those quant numbers + IV rank + your wheel history + momentum. Cached for 12 hours per ticker.

Outlooks are not investment advice. The quant model assumes returns are lognormal and stationary — it is a useful baseline, not a forecast.
